✦   Our Story

Born from a
Passion for
Pure Scent

Aroma Bliss was founded on the belief that fragrance is the most personal form of luxury.

🌹

Est. 2020

A Journey Through
Rare Botanicals

Aroma Bliss began as a small passion project in 2020 — blending fragrance oils at home, experimenting with rare resins, florals, and woods sourced from markets around the world.

What started as a personal pursuit quickly became something shared. Friends, then strangers, were drawn in by the depth and authenticity of each scent.

Today, Aroma Bliss offers a curated collection of Eau de Parfum and pure fragrance oils — each one a narrative of carefully chosen notes.

"Scent is the language of the soul. We simply help you find the right words."

— The Founder
